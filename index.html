10 Billionaire Habits to Start in Your 20s

Success starts early. These habits will set your foundation strong:

  • Wake up early and plan your day
  • Invest in yourself daily (learning/health)
  • Track every rupee and spend with purpose
  • Network with ambitious people
  • Read daily — books, finance, mindset
  • Stay fit — your body is your tool
  • Start building passive income
  • Practice gratitude and focus
  • Take calculated risks
  • Protect your time — say no often
